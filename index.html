Bee and Puppycat
Bee and PuppyCat is an original webseries created by Natasha Allegri and produced by Frederator Studios. It follows the lives of Bee, a directionless twenty-something woman, and PuppyCat, a mysterious creature that literally falls into her life one day. Together they work intergalactic temp jobs to pay the rent!
